Corduroy Velvet

Short film

Corduroy Velvet is a short music film featuring narrative elements and live performances by Emmitt James, the artist behind the EP that shares the name of this short film.

The film stars Emmitt James and Nyzeire McGee. I was the sole creator of everything not relating to audio, which was championed by musician and audio engineer Brendan Lynch-Salamon.
